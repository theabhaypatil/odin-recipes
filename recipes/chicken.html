Chicken

The day you learned to bake chicken strips.

It all starts with a spice mixture. The rest is really simple.

The spice mix will flavor the chicken. Protip: Add the spice mix to plain Greek yogurt. Use it to marinade the chicken strips

Ingredients

  • Chicken strips (duh!)
  • Cooking oil
  • Plain Greek yogurt
  • Spice mix
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lime
  • A few twigs of fresh corriander

Steps

  1. Use the spice-mix Greek yogurt, oil, salt & pepper to marinade the chicken strips. Ideally the night before.
  2. Pre-heat oven to 200C
  3. Lay the marinaded chicken on a baking sheet in a baking pan
  4. Place the bake pan inside the oven
  5. Bake at 200C for about 15-16mins
  6. Serve with a dash of lime and corriander-garnish. Enjoy!
