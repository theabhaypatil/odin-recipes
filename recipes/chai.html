Chai

The day you learned to make cardamom-flavored chai.

It all starts with the quality of milk and cardamom.

The secret is to crush the cardomom seeds into a fine powder. This will guarantee a fresh and aromatic chai every single time.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • A few fresh cardamom pods
  • Indian tea leaves/ powder
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan add about 200ml of milk per person
  2. Place the pan on med-high heat. Add Indian tea leaves/ powder to the milk
  3. Let it come to boil. Caution! The milk will spill if left unattended
  4. Lower the flame
  5. Add freshly powdered cardamom to the hot milk
  6. Add a tea-spoon of sugar (optional)
  7. Bring the milk to another boil. Caution!
  8. Now the tea is ready! Pour it in a cup using a sieve
  9. Serve and savour the absolute deliciousness!
