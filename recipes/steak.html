Steak

The day you learned to cook a perfect steak.

It all starts with the quality of the steak. The rest is really simple.

By quality, we mean the following. The more boxes you check, the better the odds of success even before you enter the kitchen

  1. Steak source (butcher shop over supermarket)
  2. Steak size (thickness not lower than 2-3 inch)
  3. Steak type (tenderness and smoothness)

Ingredients

  • Steak (duh!)
  • Cooking oil & butter
  • 2-3 pods of peeled garlic
  • 1 bayleaf
  • A few strands of fresh thyme
  • A few strands of fresh rosemary (optional)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat up a cast-iron or stainless-steel pan to medium-high. Verify via mercurial water.
  2. Add some oil so as to cover the whole surface of the pan
  3. Lay the salted and tempered steak in the pan, away from you
  4. About 2mins in take a peek underneath at the sear. If acceptable, flip it gently
  5. After about a minute, add a good knob of butter in the pan, away from the steak
  6. Add roughly smashed garlic pods, bayleaf, and herbs in the butter
  7. Start basting: use a table-spoon to rinse the steak w the flavored butter over and over
  8. After about a minute, move the steak from the pan to a (warm) plate/ wooden chopping board. Pour the pan contents over the rested steak
  9. Let it rest for about 3-5mins
  10. Slice it against the grain. Serve and savour the absolute deliciousness!
